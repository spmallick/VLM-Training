# SoberStack Consulting Reimbursement Policy

- Meals and non-alcoholic beverages are reimbursable when tied to client work, travel, or approved team events.
- Alcohol is never reimbursable and must be excluded from the claim amount.
- Any tax attributable to alcohol must also be excluded from the reimbursable amount.
- If the claimed amount is lower than the receipt total, the employee must explain the excluded charges in the adjustment note field.
- Receipts must include vendor, date, total, and itemized line items; incomplete receipts require manual review.
