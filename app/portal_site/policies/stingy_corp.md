# Stingy Corp. Reimbursement Policy

- Business travel fares, parking, tolls, and mandatory travel fees are reimbursable when supported by a valid receipt.
- Tips and gratuities are never reimbursable and must be excluded from the claim amount.
- Taxes and mandatory fees are reimbursable only when they are clearly separated from the non-reimbursable tip.
- If the claimed amount is lower than the receipt total, the employee must explain the excluded charges in the adjustment note field.
- Receipts must clearly show the fare, tax or fee breakdown, and tip line; otherwise the claim is routed to manual review that trigger a $10 fine.
